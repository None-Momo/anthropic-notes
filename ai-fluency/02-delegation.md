# Delegation

## What you'll learn
- Spot tasks that are safe and high-leverage to delegate to AI.
- Choose the right level of autonomy and oversight.
- Break work into AI-suitable chunks.

## Delegation patterns
- **Draft then refine**: AI drafts, you edit.
- **Summarize then verify**: AI condenses, you fact-check.
- **Classify then sample**: AI labels, you audit a sample.
- **Brainstorm then filter**: AI generates options, you choose.

## Risk prompts
- Impact if wrong?
- Safety/privacy constraints?
- Data quality/recency needs?
- Time saved vs. time to verify?

## Guardrails
- Provide scope, style, and forbidden content.
- Ask for sources or reasoning.
- Require step-by-step and a final concise answer.

## Practice
- Pick a current task and mark: delegate / share / keep.
