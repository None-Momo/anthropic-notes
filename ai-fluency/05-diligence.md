# Diligence

## What you'll learn
- Put lightweight oversight in place for AI-assisted work.
- Track versions, assumptions, and sign-offs.

## Practices
- **Traceability**: keep prompts, key inputs, and outputs.
- **Versioning**: label iterations and decisions taken.
- **Verification**: record what was checked (facts, safety, bias).
- **Approval**: who is accountable, who reviewed.
- **Guardrails**: redlines (privacy, security, regulated content).

## Minimal template
- Purpose & scope
- Inputs used (and their quality)
- Prompt(s) and model settings (temp, top_p if relevant)
- Checks performed and results
- Final owner & date

## Practice
- Create a one-page diligence log for a current AI-assisted task.
